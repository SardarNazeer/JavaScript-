1) words vs keyword

special word jiska ek specific meaning huta

2) variable & declaration

data ko store karne ke liye variable ka use huta hai.
var, let, const
var purana hai use nahi karenge
let jab humein bad me uski value change karne paregi.
const fix value jo change nahi hu skti
